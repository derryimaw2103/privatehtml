Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our TikTok integration app.

Information We Collect

We request only the TikTok API permissions necessary for uploading and publishing videos on your behalf. No personal information is collected outside of what is required for these operations.

How We Use Your Information

Data obtained through the TikTok API is used solely for actions you explicitly authorize, such as uploading videos. We do not share or sell your information to third parties.

Data Retention

Upload tokens and video metadata are cached only as long as needed to complete the upload. All temporary data is deleted within 24 hours.

User Controls

You may revoke access at any time through TikTok or request deletion of any residual data by emailing us.

Security

All communication occurs over HTTPS, and any temporary tokens are stored in encrypted form.
